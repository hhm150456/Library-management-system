The best TikTok trends of 2023

Aged filter

        With the help of this filter, users may give their faces wrinkles and grey hair to give 
        themselves an incrediblyrealistic glimpse of what they can look like in the future.
                
        The artificial intelligence-powered filter, which modifies users' appearances, 
        has impressed some dermatologists on its accuracy.

Beige flag, red flag, green flag

    The beige, red, and green flag trend consists of three filters that highlight the positive 
    and negative aspects of people's personalities, especially when it comes to dating.
                
    Unappealing behaviour is denoted by red flags,whilst positive features are shown 
    by green flags.
    
    Later, anything deemed somewhat bothersome, odd, or even boring– 
    like consistently asking a waiter for a recommendation or doing nothing at all while 
    flying – was marked with a beige flag.
        
    The beige filter was first popularised, according to TikTok, by French-Canadian 
    influencer Laura Gouillon.

Yearbook photo makeover

        This one is a filter that makes individuals imagine what they would look like if they 
        had a makeover from the 1990s.

        Users choose a filter, strike a stance, and then tap the screen to transform their 
        features into a yearbook portrait that resembles the headshots you could see in 
        a high school yearbook in the United States.
